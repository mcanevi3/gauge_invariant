\subsection{Example 1}
The system is given as, $A=-1$, $B_w=1$,
\begin{equation}
    C_0=\begin{bmatrix}
        1\\2
    \end{bmatrix}
    ,\,D_w=\begin{bmatrix}
        3\\3
    \end{bmatrix},\,
    H=\begin{bmatrix}
        1&0\\0&2
    \end{bmatrix},\,
    E=1
\end{equation}
The uncertainty is modeled as,
\begin{equation}
\begin{split}
    C_0+H\Delta E&=
    \begin{bmatrix}
        1\\2
    \end{bmatrix}+
    \begin{bmatrix}
        1&0\\0&2
    \end{bmatrix}
    \begin{bmatrix}
        \delta_1\\\delta_2
    \end{bmatrix}\\
    &=
    \begin{bmatrix}
        1+\delta_1\\2+2\delta_2
    \end{bmatrix}
\end{split}
\end{equation}
The projection is calculated as,
\begin{equation}
\begin{split}
    \Pi_0&=
    \begin{bmatrix}
        1&0\\0&1
    \end{bmatrix}-\begin{bmatrix}
        1\\2
    \end{bmatrix}\left(
    \begin{bmatrix}
        1&2
    \end{bmatrix}\begin{bmatrix}
        1\\2
    \end{bmatrix}\right)^{-1}
    \begin{bmatrix}
        1&2
    \end{bmatrix}\\
    &=
    \begin{bmatrix}
        0.8&   -0.4\\
        -0.4&    0.2\\
    \end{bmatrix}
\end{split}
\end{equation}
The output is explicitly written as,
\begin{equation}
\begin{split}
    y&=\begin{bmatrix}
       1+\delta_1\\2+2\delta_2
    \end{bmatrix}x+\begin{bmatrix}
        3\\3
    \end{bmatrix}w\\
\end{split}
\end{equation}
The projection is applied and the following is obtained,
\begin{equation}
\begin{split}
    \Pi_0 y&=\begin{bmatrix}
        0.8&   -0.4\\
        -0.4&    0.2\\
    \end{bmatrix}\begin{bmatrix}
       1+\delta_1\\2+2\delta_2
    \end{bmatrix}x+\begin{bmatrix}
        0.8&   -0.4\\
        -0.4&    0.2\\
    \end{bmatrix}\begin{bmatrix}
        3\\3
    \end{bmatrix}w\\
    &=\begin{bmatrix}
    0.8\delta_1 - 0.8\delta_2\\
    0.4\delta_2 - 0.4\delta_1
    \end{bmatrix}x
    +\begin{bmatrix}
        1.2\\-0.6
    \end{bmatrix}w
\end{split}
\end{equation}
Premultiplying the equation with
\begin{equation}
    (\Pi_0 H)^\dagger=\begin{bmatrix}
    0.5&   -0.25\\
   -0.5&    0.25\\
    \end{bmatrix}
\end{equation}
gives,
\begin{equation}
(\Pi_0 H)^\dagger\Pi_0y=\begin{bmatrix}
0.5\delta_1 - 0.5\delta_2\\
0.5\delta_2 - 0.5\delta_1\\
\end{bmatrix}x+\begin{bmatrix}
0.7500\\
-0.7500
\end{bmatrix}w
\end{equation}
